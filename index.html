The Pearl of the Indian Ocean

An Island of Incredible Variety

Sri Lanka is an island nation in the Indian Ocean, known as the Pearl of the Indian Ocean. Despite its small size, the country boasts an incredible variety of nature, culture, and traditions that have been shaped over thousands of years.

From pristine beaches to misty mountains, ancient cities to modern cities, Sri Lanka offers a diverse range of experiences for every type of traveler.

  • Golden beaches
  • Lush tea plantations
  • Ancient cities & temples
  • Wildlife & national parks
  • Friendly people
  • Ayurvedic wellness
